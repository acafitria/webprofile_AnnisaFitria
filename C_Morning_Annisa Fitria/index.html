About Me

Annisa Fitria, who is often called Aca, is a student majoring in Information Technology at Lambung Mangkurat University.

Since childhood, Aca has always been enthusiastic about exploring new places.

Throughout her educational journey in the field of technology, Aca discovered a strong interest in the world of graphic design.

Whether it's in the form of two-dimensional (2D) or three-dimensional (3D) design, Aca finds joy in creating inspirational visual artworks.

However, currently, her main focus is on a more specific field, namely User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design.

She believes that good design is the key to the success of products, and currently, she is nurturing her knowledge to become an expert in the innovative field of UI/UX design.
